## 🔧 Build Based on Feedback

### Priority Matrix
| Impact | Effort | Priority |
|--------|--------|----------|
| High | Low | **DO FIRST** |
| High | High | Plan next |
| Low | Low | Batch together |
| Low | High | Ignore (for now) |

### Feedback → Feature Pipeline
1. Collect feedback (in-app, email, social)
2. Tag by category (bug, feature, UX, performance)
3. Score by impact (1-10) and effort (1-10)
4. Build highest impact / lowest effort first
5. Ship weekly updates
6. Tell users who requested it when it's live
